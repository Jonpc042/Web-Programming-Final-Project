In the workspace there may be issues with other employees or work-related matters that need to be handled in a specific manner.

This page is a good starter if you're experiencing these issues such as any form of harassment or negligence in the workspace.

On the left-side column please navigate to the page you feel best suits you. Feel free to contact us for further questions or concerns.

Remember the intent of this program is to benefit the employee and the company, so do not feel embarrassed for contacting HR.

Corrective action is a program that aims to guide an employee toward better performance or behavior by identifying the problems.

A large part of corrective action is focused on finding the solution and to better the employee rather than punish.

If there isn't improvement, or the issues continue to occur then more serious action might take place.

This could range from an oral warning to the potential dismissal or demotion of an employee depending on the severity of the issue.
